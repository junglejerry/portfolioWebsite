PROFESSIONAL PROFILE

Jerry John Comellas is an energetic leader and creative programmer. A veteran of the U.S. Army, he is a self-starter and team player. His natural ability to establish positive working partnerships has led to the success of several organizations. Jerry is a hard worker and determined to complete what he starts. He is a quick learner and he enjoys teaching others what he has learned. He truly wants to make a difference in the world which shows in his volunteer work. He is the Founder and Director of Acorn to Oak, International, a missions and compassion ministries organization. Jerry is a catalyst for change and a true reformer, helping churches and ministries develop modern strategies to impact the world. Jerry enjoys playing the ukulele, kayaking and spending time with his family. He is married to Suzy and they have four children: Savannah, Sierra, Josiah, and Selah.
